Republique

“Republique” is a AAA-quality episodic stealth game I developed while employed by Darkwind Media. I was heavily involved in the mobile and Playstation aspects of development on my time on the project from June 2015 to the project's completion on March 2016.

Personal Technical Accomplishments:

  • Gameplay scripting/development, bug fixing, and performance optimization throughout all 5 episodes.
  • Re-writing the saving system to exploit multithreading and increase speed/performance by 8x without breaking/converting existing saves.
  • Porting file reading/writing, xml serialization, and third-party libraries to Playstation platforms.
