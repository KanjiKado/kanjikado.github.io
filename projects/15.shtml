15: Wall Slam

"15: Wall Slam" is an elegantly simple mobile game developed solo in 48 hours as a Ludum Dare compo entry. The goal was to create a bright, minimalistic game with an incredibly satisfying reactions to player input. I continued its development in my spare time over the course of the year and have made it available on both iOS and Android as of Fall 2016.

Personal Technical Accomplishments:

  • Implements pooling to minimize memory allocation/garbage collection.
  • Optimized physics and collision detection with reduced calculation iteration counts.
  • Dynamically resizes playing area to support ALL aspect ratios.
