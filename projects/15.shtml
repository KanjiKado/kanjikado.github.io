15

"15" is an elegantly simple mobile game developed solo in 48 hours. The goal was to create a bright, minimalistic game with an incredibly satisfying reactions to player input. I have been continuing its development in my spare time and will be releasing the game on Android and iOS by the end of the year.

Personal Technical Accomplishments:

  • Implements pooling to minimize memory allocation/garbage collection.
  • Optimized physics and collision detection with reduced calculation iteration counts.
  • Dynamically resizes playing area to support ALL aspect ratios.
