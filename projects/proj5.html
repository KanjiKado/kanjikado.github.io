Reign of the Heat-Seeking Jetpack Lawnmower

Reign of the Heat-Seeking Jetpack Lawnmower is a personal project I developed way back in 2012 with Flash. Though Flash has died out and my knowledge of its systems are no longer relevant, the project demonstrates my ability to motivate myself, focus on design, and willpower to see difficult tasks through to completion. The game was featured briefly on the frontpage of r/gaming.

Personal Technical Accomplishments:

  • Custom achievement framework and 26 unique achievements.
  • Pseudo-random and configurable blood effects.
  • Implementation of external leaderboard API.
