Dodgecube

Dodgecube is an high-octane music/rhythm game I developed in collaboration with musician Alex Lane and UI designer Gary Davis. Finished in 3 months, the final product is a polished cross-platform experience in which the player (bright cube) must avoid a variety of hazards by jumping between floating platforms.

Personal Technical Accomplishments:

  • Custom lighting solution to support mobile-performant pixel-perfect shadows from 5 active light-sources.
  • Hybrid control paradigm supports live swapping of touch, tilt, joystick, and bluetooth controller input.
  • Google Play leaderboards and 25 achievements.
