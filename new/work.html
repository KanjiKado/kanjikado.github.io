Wulverblade

Wulverblade is an action-packed and bloody 2D sidescrolling beat 'em up game for PC and consoles. I worked collaboratively toward our multi-platform release as the main development force behind the PS4 version and took charge over much of the continued bugfixes and updates across all platforms after release.

  • Deeply invested in delivering highly polished releases under tight deadlines for all 4 platforms (Switch, PS4, Xbox One, & Steam).
  • Working with platform partners to submit over 16 updates and a physical version.
  • Responsible for implementing and maintaining many key systems: PS4 platform, drop-out co-op, Japanese localization, and more.

Crane Brain 3D

Crane Brain 3D is a visual sales tablet and desktop application for Gorbel Industries that enables sales employees to quickly generate product renderings in a configurable 3D space. I was involved with many areas of app development but particularly involved with data storage/security and the art pipeline.

  • Ensuring stability and security of pricing/sales tool regularly handling transactions valued above tens of thousands of dollars.
  • Balancing capability of one-click export from application to production as well accessibility offline.
  • Responsible for "decoration" art pipeline allowing for 28+ unique realisticly-scaled decorative designed with instanced rendering in mind.

Marvel's Iron Man VR

Marvel's Iron Man VR is a PlayStation-excusive free-flying first person shooter game telling the personal and heroic story of Tony Stark in a full-length campaign. I was most involved in optimizing and budgeting memory use, performance optimization/tracking, and platform-specific features.

  • Working as a team to meet firm deadlines and stringent quality expectations to deliver milestone content directly to PlayStation.
  • Fully responsible for build-, install- and run-time systems that allow each chapter of the campaign to be fully playable prior to the complete installation of the game.
  • Creating cross-disciplinary tools and trackers used by QA to record, by art to budget, and by production to analyze the performance of the game.

République

République is "the original AAA mobile game"; an episodic, console-quality, narrative-driven stealth adventure game for mobile devices also available on consoles and PC. My work throughout the game's development includes gameplay scripting & development, bugfixing, and performance optimization across all 5 episodes.

  • Porting portions of internal and third-party systems to work on different platforms such as console and VR.
  • Creating elaborate but succinct documentation for each platform's implementation of owned systems such as save data and xml serialization.
  • Re-writing the saving system to be multithreaded and increase speed/performance by 8x without breaking/converting existing saves.
