15: Wall Slam

(2016)

15: Wall Slam is a mobile game first published it after 48 hours in development as a Ludum Dare compo entry. My goal was to create a bright, minimalistic game with an incredibly satisfying reactions to player input.

Play in-browser

  • Leaderboards and achievements on Google Play and the App Store.
  • 16 delicious hand-crafted sound effects.
  • Playing area resizes to support all phone/tablet aspect ratios.
  • Some development goals and tricks in powerpoint format I've shared in person to a few local groups.

glorb.io

(2017)

glorb.io is a prototype for a casual online game of chase. I made this project to improve my networking, node.js, build pipeline, and graphics programming skills. The client runs on Babylon.js which I successfully optimized for mobile app deployment with the intent to make a tvOS/Android TV app with PhoneGap/Cordova. Read below instructions if you want to give it a try!

Play in-browser

  • Use the link above to create a room.
  • Copy/paste the link to a friend/new tab and add '&room=(your play code)' to the url
  • Change '/play?...' to '/mobile?...' on a link with the room code to get a virtual controller you can use on your phone.
  • Use WASD to move and spacebar to toggle your light
  • Red orb tries to catch the other orbs
  • First to 3 wins!

Dodgecube

(2014)

Dodgecube is an arcade rhythm game released on the now defunct PlayStation Mobile store for PlayStation Vita. Your goal is to collect combo and powerups as you try to survive cubes, saws, and electric shocks.

View on Behance

  • Featuring 5 fresh chiptune jams courtesy of musician Alex Lane.
  • Variety of control and camera options as well as achievements and leaderboards.
  • Includes a nice easy tutorial followed by 3 ruthless stages/difficulty levels.
